Since I can remember, I've always been on the internet. From browsing various GeoCities sites and playing flash games, I went on to graduate with Computer Science degree from UCI. From then on, I gained an interest in online fiction and spent a few years doing some script programming as I worked as a translator for web fiction, reminding me of my passion for programming.

Feeling a bit rusty, I decided I needed review to get my skills up to speed and set my mind on attend a coding bootcamp named Learningfuze. While I was there, I went through twelve weeks of rigorous coursework, practicing strict git control, making several apps, and regaining my confidence.

I'm now a full stack developer in the Orange County area and looking for opportunities to prove myself.
